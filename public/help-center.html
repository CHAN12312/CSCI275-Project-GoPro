Instructions

  1. Navigate to the home page to explore the platform.
  2. Use the "Find Your Pro Gamer" button to choose a game and get matched with a pro gamer.

  5. Chat with pro gamers and other users in the chat section.
  6. View your purchased courses in the "My Courses" section.
  7. Check your purchase history in the "Purchase History" section.
  8. Contact support through the "Help Center" if you need assistance.
